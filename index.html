Biography

I am a second-year PhD student in the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, co-supervised by Prof. Ruimao Zhang and Prof. Zhen Li. Currently, I am an intern of computer vision at International Digital Economy Academy (IDEA), advised by Dr. Ailing Zeng and Prof. Lei Zhang.

My research interest lies in human-centric visual understanding, perception, and generation.
